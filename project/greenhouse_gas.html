Country Emissions Over Time

About the Visualization

The data in this visualization comes from the OECD Greenhouse Gases Dataset, which provides information about greenhouse gas emissions from different countries over time.

What the Visualization Represents: This chart shows how greenhouse gas emissions vary across countries and years. Each country is represented on the map, with the intensity of the color indicating the level of emissions.

How to Use the Visualization:

  • Click on a Country: Select a country on the map to view its emission trends over the years.
  • Use the Dropdown Filters: Choose a specific country or type of pollutant from the filters above the map.
  • Adjust the Year Slider: Move the slider to change the year and see how emissions have evolved.
