About the Visualization

This interactive visualization showcases Annual Surface Temperature Changes across different countries from 1961 to 2023.

What the Visualization Represents: The map highlights annual temperature variations for countries around the globe. Countries with no available temperature data are marked as "N/A."

How to Use the Visualization:

  • Hover Over a Country: Move your mouse over a country to view its surface temperature changes.
  • Adjust the Year Slider: Use the slider below the map to select a year and explore temperature changes for that specific year.
