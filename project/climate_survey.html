About the Visualization

This visualization uses data from a Climate Change Survey, a joint collaboration between Yale and Meta. The dataset reflects how people from different countries responded to questions about climate change.

What the Visualization Represents: These charts show how individuals from various countries perceive climate change, their beliefs, and concerns. Each visualization represents a different question from the survey.

How to Use the Visualization:

  • Filter by Response Type: Use the dropdown menu to select specific response types for analysis.
  • Sort the Data: Sort countries in ascending or descending order for better comparison.
  • Pan and Zoom: Adjust the view to focus on specific areas of the visualization.
  • Hover Over Bars: Move your mouse over the bars to view detailed information about the country and responses.
